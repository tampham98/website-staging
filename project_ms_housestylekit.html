Microsoft Commercial House Style Toolkit
This is a comprehensive Figma toolkit created to help design teams produce scalable, visually cohesive content across Microsoft's diverse product brands. While Microsoft products share core brand principles, each also has unique elements—such as distinct color systems for Azure, Dynamics, and others. To support both consistency and creative flexibility, I collaborated with a Creative Director and Production Designer to build a system that includes clear guidelines, on-page instructions, 20 ready-to-use templates with hundreds of swappable components, and a robust library of design assets — including custom backgrounds, device mockups, dimensional elements, and infographic components. The result is a unified, easy-to-use resource that enables teams to create high-quality content efficiently while staying aligned with Microsoft's brand presence.
